CHAPTERS OF SELF-HELP

BREAKING FREE FROM SELF-DESTRUCTIVE EMOTIONAL PROGRAMS

Human subconscious is filled with programs that steer our behavior and choices throughout life.

These programs originate from parents, childhood experiences and other influences we collect over time.

Some of them are positive, but most are negative. We may be aware of a few, yet the majority guide us without our conscious knowledge.

These hidden programs drive how we interact and react, pushing destructive energy or behaviors.

The first step is to uncover and acknowledge these programs.

Once recognized, we can begin to cleanse our subconscious and overwrite the negative programs.

We cannot simply delete them—doing so leaves a void—so we must replace each with a new positive program.

For example, if someone’s words or actions don’t match your expectations, it triggers an emotion of offense.

Each person is like a radio station, continuously transmitting energy through thoughts, words, and emotions to everyone in their field.

This energy is strongest close to the individual, enveloping them.

Offensive energy is negative and destructive, causing ill-feeling, breakdown of organic matter, and even disease.

The goal is to overwrite the negative offense program.

If someone’s behavior disappoints you, consciously choose understanding instead of offense.

Realize they act under their own negative subconscious programming—there is no deliberate malice.

Now aware, you can choose a reaction that heals your well-being and body.

We are all spiritual children. Treat adults as you would a small child:

If a toddler acts unpredictably, you don’t get angry. You smile, forgive, and respond with love.

Apply the same approach to adults.

Negative energy repels, while positive energy attracts.

If you dwell in negativity, others sense it and subconsciously keep their distance.

When you radiate positivity, people naturally gravitate toward you.

Despair and hopelessness push people away; joy of life pulls them in.
